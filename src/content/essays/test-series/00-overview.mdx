<span class="dropcap">T</span>his is the overview page of a test series. It exists to demonstrate how a longer essay — too long to read in one sitting — can be split into chapters that each get their own URL, while a sticky sidebar on the left keeps the whole structure visible and one click away.

A series shares a `series` slug across all its parts. The part with `part: 0` is the overview; subsequent parts go in order. The sidebar is rendered automatically from those frontmatter fields. So is the previous/next pager at the bottom of each page.
